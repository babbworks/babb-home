<p><b><a href="/workpads">Workpads</a> is a <a href="https://workpads.babb.tel">protocol</a> & mobile app for <a href="/creating">creating</a> & <a href="/sharing">sharing</a> unique business records, based on the <a href="https://www.babb.tel/bitpads/">Bitpads Standard</a>.</b></p>
<p>Launching now on <a href="/kaios">KaiOS</a>! View <a href="https://www.kaiostech.com/store/apps/?bundle_id=com.babbworks.workpads">the app</a> or try the <a href="https://workpads.app/mobile/kaios">demo</a>.</p>
<p>Read about our commitment to <a href="/feature-phones">the world of feature phones</a>.</p>
  <hr><br>
<p>The Workpads™ brand for software and hardware products is owned by Babb with all rights reserved.</p>
<p>The Workpads <a href="https://workpads.babb.tel">Open Standard</a> enables anyone to build similar or complimentary tools and services. In addition to describing ideal mobile architecture, the Standard provides for countless applications of the concept at the foundational levels of computing and transmission (binary encoding, RF signalling).</p>
<p>The <a href="https://workpads.app">workpads.app</a> site serves as a hub to demonstrate the many ways "Workpads" can be created. Try out '<a href="https://www.workpads.app/pads/demos/2025/04/05/A5.html">Basic Workpad 1</a>'!</p>
