Taking business into orbit.

From Rust Belt, to Kalahari to the Cosmos, we are pioneering digital & physical tools that help people work & learn.

We created Workwarrior to grow the ecosystem of open source productivity tools. We're now gradually building Warrior and BABBS as long-term answers to the issues of personal and industrial productivity. Every tool aims to be a Century System.

All products and projects are based on and contribute to the BASICS Protocol ("Business Assistance System"), a open source framework for services and functions, similar to an operating system.

Workpads is our first commercial project. It's a open protocol and mobile program for creating and sharing business records. Read about the project, try the KaiOS demo, or view our seed pitch.
